9 Ways to Optimise Your Digestion

    Compare your current routine with these 9 ways to optimise your digestive system

  • Chew food well to aid break down of starches and sugars
  • Leave time in between meals to allow the stomach to finish preparing the food for digestion and to clear
  • Include plenty of fresh, live food such as fresh vegetables and live yoghourt (unless dairy intolerant) in your food to supply enzymes that aid digestion
  • Eat a good range of healthy foods to give the body a varied supply of nutrients
  • Avoid an excess of animal products and saturated fats which overwork the liver and gall bladder
  • ?Avoid an excess of sugar and refined carbohydrate foods, such as white bread, white flour, cakes and biscuits as these put a strain on the pancreas and can cause blood sugar problems
  • Eat plenty of fibre and drink 6-8 glasses of water a day to enable the intestines to function well
  • Limit alcohol and avoid cigarette smoke as these can damage digestive function
  • Exercise regularly to keep good muscle tone that aids digestion
